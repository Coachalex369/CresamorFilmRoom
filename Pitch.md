Cresamor is a platform built for athletes to take ownership of their performance. Today, athletes rely on coaches or scattered tools to access and share their game film, and they rarely have control over the moments that define them. Cresamor changes that by allowing coaches to upload full film while athletes create and save their own highlight clips directly to their profiles. Instead of just watching film, athletes build their identity through it. Cresamor is built around one idea: your film, your way.

Future Goals "the stretch"
